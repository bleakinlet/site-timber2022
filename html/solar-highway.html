Solar Highway

the motw-like ttrpg about friendship in your space-traveling RV


A 100-page manual on a lighthearted MotW-like TTRPG I developed with my friends over at the Cryptid Friendclub. The game takes place on the Solar Highway, a road that travels through the universe and stops at the weirdest places.

Solar Highway is a full GM system that works well with a casual group. It has 12 unique passenger types you can pick from with custom abilities, moves, futures, and stories.

This game was designed from the ground up to be played weekly for my group, with people coming in and leaving. Players who can't make it can easily just leave their passenger in the RV. Players can have multiple passengers that they can pick and choose to adventure in new locations. It is designed for simple one-shot sessions, although I've done full campaigns and long, long character arcs.

Go explore that messed up universe, the RV isn't waiting!
